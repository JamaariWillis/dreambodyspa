Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy for No show, Reschedule, and Cancelled appointment. Please appreciate that when you forget or cancel your appointment without giving enough notice, or cancel and reschedule appointments repeatedly, we miss the opportunity to schedule another client, and incur cost to make staff available to provide the service.

If you cannot make your appointment time, we require a minimum of 48 hours notice of your scheduled time simply by replying “Yes” or “No”, to the confirmation text that is sent via email and text 48 hours in advance, or calling Dream Body Spa at 803-522-2498.

For New Clients

Appointments cancelled within 48 hours of the scheduled time will incur a cancellation fee equal to 100% of the appointment cost, and will be charged to the card on file. If there is no card on file, no reschedules will be authorized unless the cancellation fee is paid in full. Any deposit paid to book the original appointment is forfeited and is not applied to the cancellation fee.

If you reschedule an appointment more than once, you will be required to pay a non-refundable deposit for 100% of the full price of the service requested before scheduling a future appointment. There is no deposit refund if that future appointment is cancelled or rescheduled.

For arrivals running more than 15 minutes late for an appointment, the appointment will need to be rescheduled and require a non-refundable deposit for 100% of the full price of the service requested before scheduling a future appointment.

If you do not call or show up for a scheduled appointment, no rescheduling will be allowed. If there are extenuating circumstances, a reschedule may be allowed with 100% non-refundable payment for the rescheduled session. NO EXCEPTIONS.

For Current Clients

For prepaid sessions from a package purchase, if you cancel or reschedule less than 48-hours of the appointment, or do not show up for a prepaid session, you forfeit a session. In addition, scheduling options will be limited if there are repeat occurrences.

For All Clients

There are no refunds for services purchased. The Dream Body Spa team is committed to ensuring customers receive results, with the understanding clients will have minimal schedule adjustments to ensure the best outcome.
