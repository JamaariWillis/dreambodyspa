Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy for No show, Reschedule, and Cancelled appointment. Please appreciate that when you forget or cancel your appointment without giving enough notice, or cancel and reschedule appointments repeatedly, we miss the opportunity to schedule another client, and incur cost to make staff available to provide the service.

If you cannot make your appointment time, we ask that you let us know within 24 hours of your scheduled time simply by replying to the confirmation text that is sent out 48 hours in advance, or calling Dream Body Spa at 803-522-2498.

Appointments cancelled less than 24 hours of the scheduled time and/or No shows will incur a cancellation fee equal to $30 and will be charged to the card on file. No reschedules will be authorized unless the cancellation fee is paid in full. Any deposits paid will be forfeited and applied to the cancellation fee.

We require a non-refundable deposit for 50% of the service requested before scheduling a future appointment. If you reschedule an appointment more than once, you will be required to pay a non-refundable deposit for 50% of the full price of the service requested before scheduling a future appointment. There is no deposit refund if that future appointment is cancelled or rescheduled.

If you cancel a prepaid session in less than 24 hours, there is a cancellation fee of $30 payable by the next appointment, or forfeit of a session.
