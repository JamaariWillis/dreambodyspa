Facial Aftercare Instructions

A good at-home skincare routine is important to maximize your facial results. A good skincare routine involves cleansing, toning, moisturizing, and protecting your skin from the sun with a minimum SPF of 20 (Ideally 50).

For best results, it is recommended to:

  • Avoid waxing, shaving, scrubs, and exfoliating products for at least 48 hours.
  • Avoid artificial tanning and exposing your skin to direct sunlight. It is essential to protect your skin with SPF (Ideally 50, minimum 30).
  • Stay hydrated - this is essential for healthy skin function and improves our skin barrier as well as collagen production.
  • Stay away from direct heat sources such as saunas and vigorous exercise for 24 hours. If showering/bathing, make sure the water is warm and not hot.
  • Do not pick at your skin!
Normal side effects you may experience
  • Skin Warmth
  • Skin dryness
  • Skin tightness
  • Itching sensation
  • Erythema in the area of application
Treatment Aftercare Instructions

Treatment Aftercare Instructions

It is recommended to follow a balanced lifestyle with a healthy diet and exercise routine for best results. Please also ensure you are remaining hydrated.

If desired, you may incorporate complementary detox treatments into your routine such as:

  • Infrared sauna
  • Whole body cryotherapy
  • Lymphatic Drainage massage
  • Compression

For best results these treatments should be done 24 hours after your shape treatment.

Normal side effects you may experience in the treatment area for up to 24 hours post treatment:
  • Numbing
  • Soreness
  • Localized redness and raised tissue
  • Skin Warmth
  • Skin dryness
  • Skin tightness
  • Itching sensation
